About

User behaviour is the material of design.

I am a multi-disciplinary designer and Design Lead at PowerLabs , where my recent work is centred on creating a coherent energy ecosystem that works for Enterprise and energy consumers.

My work lives at the edges: where hardware meets interface, where data becomes decision, where a system's inner logic has to surface without noise.

Alongside product work, I am always keeping one eye on the overlap between strategy, systems, and design craft.

I enjoy night walks and creating personal playlists for loved ones. Currently watching THE BOYS and reading Build by Tony Fadell.
